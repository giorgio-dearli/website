how it works.

see where your time goes

your dearli score shows you who you're actually staying close to — and where you might be drifting. awareness is the first step.

turn intentions into plans

"we should hang out" becomes an actual date on the calendar. dearli makes scheduling easy so you actually follow through.

share the work

everyone can suggest times and places. the invisible labor of planning gets distributed instead of falling on one person.

stay close over time

build rhythms that stick. get gentle nudges when someone's slipping. your relationships stay healthy without constant effort.
